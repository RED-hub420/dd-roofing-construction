Services

We cover roofing, general construction, concrete, and interior remodeling. Here's how each service category breaks down and what kinds of jobs we typically handle.

Roofing

Roofing

Roofing is the core of what we do. We install and repair residential roofs every week across North Texas. Whether you've had a slow leak for two years or a storm rolled through last week and tore up your shingles, we can assess the damage, give you an honest scope, and get the work done.

We work primarily with asphalt shingles on residential homes, which is the most common roof type in this area. We'll walk you through material options, explain what needs to come off versus what can be repaired, and give you a price that reflects the actual job — not an inflated number padded for negotiation.

If you've had storm damage and are dealing with an insurance claim, we can work with you through that process. We document the damage clearly and work with adjusters, but we don't fabricate damage or promise specific payouts. We tell you what we actually see and what it costs to fix it.

  • Full roof replacement — tear-off and new install
  • Roof repair — partial sections, damaged areas
  • Storm damage repair — hail, wind, fallen debris
  • Leak diagnosis and repair
  • Flashing repair and replacement
  • Shingle repair and color matching
  • Flat roof and low-slope roofing
  • Ridge cap, ventilation, and underlayment work

Construction

General Construction

Beyond roofing, we operate as a general contractor for residential and light commercial projects. That means we can handle structural framing, additions, build-outs, and other construction work that goes beyond a single trade.

If you're adding a room, building out a garage, finishing a commercial space, or have structural repairs that need real carpentry work — this is where we fit. We approach these jobs the same way we approach roofing: figure out what it actually takes, give you a real number, and do the work right.

  • Structural framing — new construction and repair
  • Room additions and expansions
  • Garage conversions and build-outs
  • Commercial tenant improvements
  • Shop and outbuilding construction
  • Structural repairs after water or storm damage
  • General carpentry and repairs
  • Subfloor and decking work
Concrete & Exterior

Concrete & Exterior Work

We pour concrete for residential and commercial jobs — plain slabs, decorative finishes, patios, driveways, extensions, and shop pads. If you have an existing slab that needs to be matched or extended, we can work to it.

North Texas soil can be hard on concrete over time. Whether you need a new pour from scratch or are dealing with cracking and settling in an existing slab, we assess the situation and give you a straight answer on what makes sense to fix versus replace. We don't recommend a full pour when a repair will hold.

  • Concrete slabs — shops, garages, outbuildings
  • Driveways — new pour and replacement
  • Patio slabs and extensions
  • Decorative concrete finishes
  • Sidewalks and walkways
  • Flatwork for residential and commercial
  • Slab repair and crack remediation
Interior

Interior Remodeling

We do interior remodeling work for homeowners who want to update a kitchen, repair or replace drywall and sheetrock, or take on a broader interior renovation. We're not a design firm — we're a crew that does the actual work cleanly and on budget.

Most interior remodel jobs come to us through word of mouth, often after a homeowner has already had a roofing or construction job done with us. We're happy to coordinate with flooring, tile, and finish contractors you've already hired — or point you toward people we know do good work locally.

  • Kitchen updates — cabinets, counters, layout changes
  • Drywall and sheetrock installation and repair
  • Texture matching and wall repair
  • Interior doors, trim, and finish carpentry
  • Bathroom updates and remodeling
  • Interior painting
  • Water damage repair and restoration
