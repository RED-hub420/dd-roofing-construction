About D&D Roofing & Construction

A North Texas contractor built around practical work, honest communication, and getting the job done right.

Who We Are

D&D Roofing & Construction is a general contractor based in North Texas. We work across the DFW area on residential and light commercial projects, with roofing as our primary trade and general construction, concrete, and interior remodeling rounding out what we do.

We're a working contractor — not a lead-generation service, not a franchise, not a large company with a sales team. When you call D&D, you're talking to the people who will actually show up and do the work.

Most of our jobs come from repeat customers and referrals. That's not an accident. We'd rather do solid work for people who recommend us than chase volume and cut corners. In a market like North Texas, where a busy storm season means a flood of fly-by-night crews rolling through, there's real value in being a contractor your neighbors have already used and trust.

How We Approach the Work

We start every project by seeing the job in person. We don't give quotes over the phone for work we haven't looked at. Once we've assessed the scope, we put together a written estimate that covers what we're doing, what materials we're using, and what it costs. That number is what you pay — we don't come back after the job is done with additions that weren't in the original scope.

We communicate throughout the job. If something unexpected comes up — a rotted deck board under the shingles, a framing issue behind drywall, a drainage problem under a slab — we call before we proceed. You make the call on how to handle it.

When the job is done, we clean up. That means hauling off debris, clearing the work area, and walking you through what was done. We've heard too many stories from homeowners who hired a crew and came home to a mess left in the yard. That's not how we operate.

What We Hold Ourselves To

✓

Straight estimates. We scope the job, give you the number, and stick to it unless the scope changes — and if it does, we talk to you first.

✓

Showing up when we say we will. We know your time matters. If something changes our schedule, we call ahead.

✓

Cleaning up after ourselves. The job site should look better when we leave than when we got there.

✓

Standing behind the work. If something we built or repaired fails early, we come back and make it right.

✓

Honest scope. We tell you what the job actually needs, not the version that's easiest to sell.
